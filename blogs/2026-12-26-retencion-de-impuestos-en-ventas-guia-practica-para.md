Tax retention on online catalog sales through Clip applies differently by taxpayer status: persons without RFC face 16% IVA and 20% ISR retention, while those with RFC or legal entities have reduced rates. Clip acts as a withholding agent (agente recaudador) to help businesses comply with Mexican tax authorities.
